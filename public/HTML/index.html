CRAVIO


cheesecake

A dessert consisting of a crust made from crushed cookies or graham crackers and a filling made from cream cheese, sugar, and eggs. It is typically baked and can be flavored with various ingredients such as fruit, chocolate, or caramel. The texture is creamy and smooth, and it is often served chilled as a sweet and indulgent treat.

icecream

A frozen dessert made from a mixture of cream, sugar, and flavorings. The mixture is churned to incorporate air, which gives its characteristic texture and consistency. It can be made in a variety of flavors and is typically served cold, either on its own or as a topping for other desserts. Ice cream is a popular sweet treat enjoyed all over the world.

cookies

Baked treats made from a mixture of flour, sugar, eggs, and butter (or other fats) along with flavorings such as chocolate chips, nuts, or dried fruits. Typically formed into small, flat rounds or other shapes, which are then baked until golden brown. Cookies are a popular dessert or snack enjoyed all over the world, and there are countless variations on the basic recipe.
